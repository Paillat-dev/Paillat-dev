FABLE

FABLE is a This repository containing a software that connects a whole bunch of scripts and tools together to automate the process of generatig unlimited youtube videos from a given channel subject/theme. It handles everything from creating the script, to generating the video, to uploading it to YouTube as well as creating the thumbnail and the description. The only needed manual work is to make the video public on the YouTube Studio interface, because YouTube doesn't allow to do it with the open tier of their API.
